Travel Tips

Everything you need to know before you go

📅

Book Early

November is high season in Puerto Vallarta. Book flights and accommodations as soon as possible to secure the best rates and availability. Consider adding Priority boarding for easier travel.

🚕

Transportation

Use official airport taxis or arrange private transfers through the hotel (~$25-30). Zona Romántica is walkable, but taxis and Ubers are affordable for longer distances.

💵

Currency

Mexico uses pesos (MXN). Many places accept USD, but paying in pesos usually gives better rates. Bring cash for tips and markets. ATMs are widely available.

🛂

Documentation

Ensure passports are valid for at least 6 months beyond travel dates. U.S. citizens don't need visas for tourism, but always check current entry requirements before departure.

💧

Health & Safety

Puerto Vallarta is generally safe. Stay aware at night, drink bottled water, use reef-safe sunscreen, and stay hydrated in the tropical climate.

📱

Stay Connected

Check with your carrier about international plans. The resort offers complimentary Wi-Fi. Consider purchasing a local SIM card for better data coverage around the city.
