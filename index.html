Puerto Vallarta 2025

Your Perfect November Escape
📍 Recommended: November 9-16, 2025 (7 nights)

🎯 Our Top Recommendation

Secrets Vallarta Bay (Option 1 or 4) delivers the best value for four 26-year-olds seeking a social, party-friendly atmosphere. Book through Costco Travel for shop cards and transfers, or direct through Hyatt for potential $400 savings if you book by October 2nd. The resort attracts younger couples, features swim-up bars, themed party nights, and creates the vibrant energy your group wants—all within your $1,500/person budget.

🏨 Alternative: Boutique Hotels in Romantic Zone

For a completely different experience, consider boutique hotels in Puerto Vallarta's Romantic Zone (Zona Romántica). Instead of an all-inclusive resort, you'll stay in a walkable urban neighborhood with 100+ restaurants, vibrant nightlife, and authentic Mexican culture—all steps from Los Muertos Beach.

Best budget option: V Azul condos ($140-210 per person) with full kitchens let you save $200-400 per person on food while staying in the heart of the action. You'll have $1,100+ remaining per person for amazing dinners, activities, and nightlife.

Social atmosphere: Casa Cupula ($490-770 pp) or Almar Resort ($770-1,050 pp) offer legendary pool parties and LGBT+-friendly vibes perfect for meeting other travelers.
